How to reach the University campus:

Depending on your location in Paris/Banlieue, you can:
- take RER C; get off at the "Épinay-sur-Seine" station; take the tram T11, direction "Le Bourget"; get off at the "Villetaneuse-Université" station.
- take RER D; get off at the "Pierrefitte-Stains" station; take the tram T11, direction "Épinay-sur-Seine"; get off at the "Villetaneuse-Université" station.
- take transilien H; get off at the "Épinay-Villetaneuse" station; take the tram T11, direction "Le Bourget"; get off at the "Villetaneuse-Université" station.
- take métro 13; get off at the "Saint Denis-Porte de Paris" station; take the tram T8, direction "Villetaneuse-Université"; get off at the "Villetaneuse-Université" station.

In all the cases above you will enter the University campus from the "north entrance", just next to the station and easily recognisable (or just follow the students).
Finally, this map of the University campus may help you (with the notations of the map, you enter from "entrée nord 2").
