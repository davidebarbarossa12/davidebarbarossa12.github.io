Currently

Since 01/2024, I am a post-doctoral Research Associate in the Mathematical foundations of computation group of the the Department of Computer Science of the University of Bath.
I work within Thomas Powell's EPSRC project Imperative Programs from Proofs.

Previously

  • From 03/2022 to 01/2024, I was a post-Doc researcher at the Dipartimento di Informatica of Università di Bologna (the oldest still active university in the western world!).
    I worked within Ugo Dal Lago's ERC CoG project Differential Program Semantics (DIAPASoN).

  • In 12/2021, I obtaned a PhD in Theoretical Computer Science at the Laboratoire d'Informatique de Paris Nord of Université Sorbonne Paris Nord and the Dipartimento di Matematica of Università di Roma Tre, with supervisors: Giulio Manzonetto and Lorenzo Tortora de Falco.
    Have a look at the dedicated page.
